Walking, 2019: Ambulation Appreciation. Meditation on a common movement.

Milwaukee: April 7, 2020. Made in response to the uncertainty and concern that arose when the state of Wisconsin, led by a divided state government, held an important election while federal stay-at-home orders were in place due to the COVID-19 pandemic. There was a sense of turbulence, distrust, and injustice in Milwaukee as people made their way to the crowded polls or chose to stay home out of concern for personal and public health.

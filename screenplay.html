ScreenPlay

ScreenPlay (2019) is a computer instrument that uses light as its only input. Made with Max/MSP and utilizing pixel-tracking technology, the participant is able to "play" the screen like an instrument with nothing more than their movements and a computer with a webcam and speaker.

Some areas of the screen respond to vertical movements, others respond to horizontal ones. When activated with movement, each direction emits a unique color and sound. The velocity of the user's movements also dictate the sound's frequency and amplitude.

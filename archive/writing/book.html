Notes on a Habitat: Art and Writing From Milwaukee's Built Environment, 2022

"How do our built environments affect us? In Notes on a Habitat, Emerson reflects on how this expansive question manifests in her daily life. Through writing and visual art, she explores its answer from a deeply personal perspective.
Emerson created this collection during the 2021-2022 academic year, her final year of undergraduate study at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, while living out of a vehicle."
